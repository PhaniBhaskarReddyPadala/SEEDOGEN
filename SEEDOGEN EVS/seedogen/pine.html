Uses of Pine Seeds for Consumers:

Pine seeds, also known as pine nuts, are a versatile ingredient that can be used in various dishes. Some common uses include:

  • Adding pine nuts to salads for a delightful crunch
  • Incorporating them into pesto sauce for a nutty flavor
  • Sprinkling them on pasta and rice dishes for added texture and taste

Environmental Benefits of Pine Trees:

Pine trees, part of the Pinus genus, play a crucial role in forest ecosystems. They support wildlife habitats and contribute to the timber industry. Sustainable pine nut harvesting promotes forest conservation.
