Uses of Tulsi Seeds for Consumers:

Tulsi seeds, also known as Holy Basil seeds, have various uses and health benefits. Some common applications include:

  • Soaking seeds for a nutritious drink
  • Sprouting for salads and sandwiches
  • Growing Tulsi plants for spiritual and medicinal purposes

Environmental Benefits of Tulsi Plants:

Tulsi plants, Ocimum tenuiflorum, have cultural significance and contribute to ecological balance. They are valued for their medicinal properties and are often grown for their positive impact on the environment.
