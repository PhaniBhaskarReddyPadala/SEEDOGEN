Uses of Watermelon Seeds for Consumers:

Watermelon seeds are a nutritious snack and can be used in various ways. Some common uses include:

  • Roasting for a crunchy, protein-rich snack
  • Adding to salads and smoothies for extra nutrition
  • Planting to grow your own watermelon at home

Environmental Benefits of Watermelon Plants:

Watermelon plants, Citrullus lanatus, contribute to healthy soil and provide a refreshing and hydrating fruit. Growing watermelons at home can reduce the carbon footprint associated with transportation.
