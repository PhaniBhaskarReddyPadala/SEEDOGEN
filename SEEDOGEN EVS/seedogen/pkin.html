Uses of Pumpkin Seeds for Consumers:

Pumpkin seeds are a versatile and nutritious snack that can be enjoyed in various ways by consumers. Some common uses include:

  • Snacking on whole pumpkin seeds
  • Using them in culinary dishes, both savory and sweet
  • Blending into pumpkin seed butter for spreads and dips

Environmental Benefits of Pumpkin Plants:

Pumpkin plants, Cucurbita pepo, contribute to biodiversity and have sustainable harvesting practices that benefit local communities and the environment.
