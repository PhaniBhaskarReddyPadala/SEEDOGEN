Uses of Guava Seeds for Consumers:

Guava seeds are versatile and can be utilized in various ways. Some common uses include:

  • Blending seeds into smoothies for added nutrition
  • Drying and grinding for herbal teas
  • Growing guava plants for home gardening

Environmental Benefits of Guava Trees:

Guava trees, Psidium guajava, contribute to the environment by providing fruit with high nutritional value and supporting biodiversity. They are part of sustainable agriculture practices.
