Uses of Neem Seeds for Consumers:

Neem seeds are known for their medicinal properties and have various uses. Some common applications include:

  • Extracting oil for skincare and haircare
  • Using powdered neem seeds in herbal remedies
  • Incorporating into gardening for natural pest control

Environmental Benefits of Neem Trees:

Neem trees, Azadirachta indica, are environmentally friendly and contribute to sustainable practices. They have natural insect-repellent properties and support ecological balance.
