Uses of Lemon Seeds for Consumers:

Lemon seeds are a versatile and nutritious addition to various dishes. Some common uses include:

  • Adding a crunchy texture to salads
  • Using them in baking for a hint of citrus flavor
  • Grounding into a powder for seasoning and marinades

Environmental Benefits of Lemon Trees:

Lemon trees, Citrus limon, contribute to a healthy environment and can be part of sustainable agriculture. They provide benefits to the ecosystem and support local communities.
