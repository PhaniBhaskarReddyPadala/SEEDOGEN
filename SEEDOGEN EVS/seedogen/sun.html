Uses of Sunflower Seeds for Consumers:

Sunflower seeds are a versatile and nutritious snack that can be enjoyed in various ways by consumers. Some common uses include:

  • Snacking on whole sunflower seeds
  • Adding them to salads and dishes for added flavor and crunch
  • Using them in baking or as a topping for bread and pastries

Environmental Benefits of Sunflower Plants:

Sunflower plants, Helianthus annuus, have sustainable harvesting practices that benefit local communities and the environment.
