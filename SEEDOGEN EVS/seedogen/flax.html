Uses of Flax Seeds for Consumers:

Flax seeds are a highly nutritious seed that can be incorporated into various dishes. Some common uses include:

  • Adding ground flax seeds to oatmeal or yogurt for extra fiber
  • Using flax seeds as an egg substitute in baking for vegans
  • Sprinkling flax seeds on salads and smoothies for a nutty flavor

Environmental Benefits of Flax Plants:

Flax, Linum usitatissimum, is a versatile plant known for its fiber production. It is used in textiles and linen production. Flax cultivation is relatively eco-friendly and supports sustainable agriculture.
