Uses of Mango Seeds for Consumers:

Mango seeds are versatile and can be utilized in different ways. Some common uses include:

  • Grounding seeds into a powder for culinary applications
  • Extracting oil for skincare and haircare
  • Growing mango plants for home gardening

Environmental Benefits of Mango Trees:

Mango trees, Mangifera indica, contribute to the environment by providing oxygen and supporting biodiversity. They also play a role in sustainable agriculture and can be part of agroforestry practices.
