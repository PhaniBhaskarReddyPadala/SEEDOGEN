Uses of Walnuts for Consumers:

Walnuts are a versatile and nutritious nut that can be enjoyed in various ways by consumers. Some common uses include:

  • Snacking on whole walnuts
  • Using them in culinary dishes, both savory and sweet
  • Chopping and adding to salads or baked goods

Environmental Benefits of Walnut Trees:

Walnut trees, Juglans regia, have sustainable harvesting practices that benefit local communities and the environment.
