Uses of Cashews for Consumers:

Cashews are a versatile and delicious nut that can be enjoyed in various ways by consumers. Some common uses include:

  • Snacking on whole cashews
  • Using them in culinary dishes, both savory and sweet
  • Blending into cashew butter for spreads and dips

Environmental Benefits of Cashew Trees:

Cashew trees, Anacardium occidentale, contribute to biodiversity and soil conservation. They play a role in supporting the ecosystem and have sustainable harvesting practices that benefit local communities.
